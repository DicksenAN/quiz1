Mojokerto City

Mojokerto City is my birthplace. It is a small but bustling city, filled with history, yet progresses ever so onward.


Mojokerto is located around 40 km southwest of Surabaya. This city is one of the component units of the Surabaya metropolitan area known as Gerbangkertosusila. Mojokerto is famous for its onde-onde and its tie with Majapahit kingdom.
